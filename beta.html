Why a beta?

HELP US CATCH BUGS

Tremolator V2 is pretty much done, but there's only two of us — so there's always something we miss. A few extra people testing it in real projects catches way more than we can alone.

What's useful: bug reports, weird edge cases, the mix where it doesn't sit right, the CPU spike in a DAW we didn't try. Anything that feels off.

In return you get the final V2 free, your name in the credits if you want it, and you can talk to us directly.

Who we're looking for
  • ★ Active producers, engineers, or sound designers
  • ★ Comfortable filing structured bug reports
  • ★ Available for at least 4 weeks of testing
  • ★ Will use Tremolator on real work — not just demo it
