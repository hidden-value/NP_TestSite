Why a beta?

SHIP RIGHT OR DON'T SHIP

Tremolator V2 is more than ready to be sold. But every plugin we put out has to clear one bar — it has to survive a hundred sessions with real producers who don't owe us anything. That's what this beta is for.

We're not looking for hype. We're looking for honest feedback. Bug reports. Edge cases. The mix where it doesn't quite sit right and you can't figure out why. The CPU spike on the one DAW we forgot to test. The stuff we miss.

In return, you get the final V2 licence free, your name in the credits (if you want it), and a direct line to the team building the next plugins. Worth it.

Who we're looking for
  • ★ Active producers, engineers, or sound designers
  • ★ Comfortable filing structured bug reports
  • ★ Available for at least 4 weeks of testing
  • ★ Will use Tremolator on real work — not just demo it

"We'd rather ship Tremolator V2 three months late than ship it with a bug we could have caught. The beta isn't a marketing exercise — it's a quality gate."
