ABOUT

Two people in Sofia making audio plugins and hardware. That's the whole company.

Our story

JUST TWO OF US

We're two people who make music and got tired of fighting our own tools. So we started building the ones we wanted instead.

Tremolator was the first — a tremolo plugin with five knobs and no menus, the way we wanted it. After that we want to try hardware too: Eurorack and small standalone boxes.

No investors, no big plan. We build stuff in our spare time, put it out when it's good, and keep it working. That's it.

A few things that are true
01
No latency

Our plugins don't add latency unless you turn something on that needs it. Your playing stays in time.

02
Pay once

Buy it, own it. No subscriptions, no login, no licence server. It lives on your machine and works offline.

03
We keep it working

We fix bugs and keep old versions running. If it worked when you bought it, we want it to keep working.

"We make the stuff we wished existed, then put it out. What you do with it is up to you."
