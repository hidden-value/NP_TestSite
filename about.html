ABOUT

Tools that disappear into your work — and an independent studio that intends to keep it that way for as long as it takes.

Our story

BUILT FROM STUDIO TIME

null.point audio started where most good tools start — in a studio, at 2am, with someone cursing at a plugin. We were tired of menus, tired of preset libraries we'd never open, tired of subscriptions that felt like rent on something we'd already paid for.

So we started building. Quietly, slowly, on our own time and our own money. Tremolator was first — five parameters, no menus, the tremolo we'd always wished existed. Then came the rest of the roadmap: more plugins, then hardware, then a workshop where every unit gets built by hand.

We're a small team in Sofia. We don't have investors and we don't intend to. We ship when things are right, and we support what we ship. Indefinitely.

What we stand for
01
Zero compromise on latency

Every plugin we ship is zero-latency. No lookahead unless you explicitly enable it. Your playing stays in time, always. No exceptions.

02
One price, forever

You pay once. You own it. No subscriptions, no cloud auth, no licence servers. Download, install, use — it's yours, on your machine, offline.

03
Built to last

We support old versions. We document everything. If a plugin worked in 2024, it works in 2030. That's the commitment — not a marketing line.

"The best audio tools disappear into your work. You don't think about them. They don't fight you. You reach for the knob, it does what you expect, and you keep moving."
